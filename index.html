ULTRA ELECTRO MAGNETIC POP JAM-BOREE!

A 90's Pinoy Rock (Tunog Kalye)-inspired event, based on the iconic band Eraserhead's debut album of the same title, which provided our generation's life sountrack.

Come as you are...or in your best 90's outfit.

Join us in Mallberry Suites, 10th floor on Saturday, August 29, 2020.
Registration is from 6-7 PM. Event Proper is from 7-11 PM.
See schedule of events in Program.

This will also commemorate SMS Batch '95 Unwonted Breed 's 25th Reunion.

FREE - only for Batch 1995 and former faculty/staff (and their +1).

All Other Alumni - ₱250 only!!!
(We regret that we are unable to accomodate alumni spouses and/or kids at this event.)

Registration fee includes:

  • Food (buffet style) and a drink
  • Beer or alcoholic beverages (while supplies last)
  • Cake (Sir Roland Rivera's infamous mango cake!) + coffee/tea
  • A raffle ticket (check out our awesome Prizes)
  • And a complimentary "swag" bag filled with nostalgic and theme-appropriate items

There will be live band entertainment featuring famed local band INSIDE CITY.

Hosted by local DJ personality Bernie BitokBitok.

Official Photography by Armand Ansaldo.

Inside City

Inside City was formed in 2018, formerly known as Nobela. They were featured last year in ABS-CBN's newest talent reality show Your Moment!

Band Members: Clyde Aquino - Vocalist, Mark Chester Crisostomo - Guitarist/ Back-up Vocals, Allan Satore - Bassist Back-up Vocals, Kris Peñero - Keyboardist/ Back-up Vocals, Jay Mark Crisostomo - Drummer Back-up Vocals.
